Most Notable Features

  • Full nonlinear static and dynamic analysis of 3D models including buckling and post-buckling analysis.
  • Intelligent modeling of steel and composite structures including detailing of complicated sections and automatic formation of plastic hinges for steel frames.
  • Intelligent modeling of reinforced concrete structures including full detailing of longitudinal reinforcement, stirrups, and pre-stressing tendons.
  • Earthquake, blast, impact, vehicle, pressure source, uniform and hydrostatic pressure loading of structures.
  • Staged construction and demolition simulation.
  • Crack initiation and propagation until collapse.
  • Automatic consideration of impact from falling debris with adjacent structures.
  • Stress, strain, and kinematic contours and internal force diagrams.
  • Analysis output simulation in the form of animated charts, or movie format.
  • Ability to pause and resume the analysis.
  • Solution backup creation.
  • Boundary Conditions addition/removal during the analysis.
  • Elements/Springs addition/removal during analysis.
  • Detailed reports generation of both modeling inputs and properties, and output results.
  • Enhanced filtration options.
  • Show/hide parts of the model.
  • User defined material model which enables the user to define any material through its stress-strain relationship.
