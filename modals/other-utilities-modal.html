  • Create Region on Segments
    ELS V11 introduces a new utility that allows users to create 8-Node regions on segments of a hybrid polyline. This is specially useful when working with multiplanar complex geometries and requiring a precise definition of a region's boundaries, for later use in changing spring materials along a certain path, or removing them entirely and thus inducing a crack.
